Learning to Factorize and Relight a City

ECCV 2020

Changing Sky Illumination

We present results for relighting stacks. This can be done by copying the time-varying representation (which encodes our lighting context and sun azimuth) from a source scene and relighting a target scene's permanent representation (reflectance and geometry). We show the same randomly sampled set of illuminations applied to many different scenes across many different cities. Selecting a button below will change all of the scenes' illuminations simultaneously.
